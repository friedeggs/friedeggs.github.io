Sep 15, 2024 #academia

Due to personal and academic reasons, I have moved from Stanford University to the University of Toronto.

Neither school accepted a transfer, so I reapplied to the computer science PhD program at the University of Toronto and dropped out of the computer science PhD program at Stanford University. I am grateful to the University of Toronto administration for allowing me to submit the application off-cycle, as well as thankful to everyone who helped me along the way. I will miss everyone I had the fortune to meet or cross paths with at Stanford University. I have started as a first year CS PhD student at the University of Toronto and look forward to the journey to come. I will work on self-driving vehicles under the excellent supervision of Prof. Raquel Urtasun who I previously worked with during and immediately following undergrad. I am quite excited about this collaboration and hope you will come to share my excitement too. :)
